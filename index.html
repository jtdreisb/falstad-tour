    Adding Components: Simple Diode Circuit

    Here is a demonstration of how to add components to your circuit
    Your screen should be blank, now to add some components:
    1: move your cursor to any black space, right-click --> Inputs/Outputs --> Add Voltage Source (2-Terminal)
    2: click and drag to where you want your voltage source; if you don't like your placement, you can right-click in the black space and click "Select/Drag Selected" so you can move your component
    3: Next add a diode (in the Active Components list) and a resistor to your circuit in series
    4: To connect your components, you can hit "w" on your keyboard, or right-click and click "wire"; Now simply click and drag where you want your wires to go

    You are now able to add components to any circuit; you can always delete any components on your circuit by right-clicking on it and clicking "delete" in the menu

    Exporting Circuits: JFET Amplifier

    Here is a demonstration on how to export the circuits you have made
    We will export this example JFET Amplifier

    After you are done with this circuit (adding components, moving pieces around, etc...), you are ready to export:
    1: move your cursor to any black space in the area where you design your circuits
    2: right-click --> File --> Export
    3: highlight and copy all the information that pops up in the export window, then click "Close"

    Now you are ready to import, learn how on the next page

    Importing Circuits: Common BJT Emitter Amplifier

    Here is a demonstration on how to import the circuits you have made

    Now that you have the exported information copied, you are ready to import:
    1: This screen is blank, but we want our JFET Amplifier circuit, so move your cursor to a black area
    2: right-click --> File --> Import
    3: paste the information into the import box and click "Import"

    Now your circuit should appear in the circuit design, just as you had it before, ready to be edited once again
